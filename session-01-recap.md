## Session Recap

```
=== SESSION RECAP — SESSION 1 ===
DANGER ROLL RESULT: Low-Moderate (appropriate for session 1 
                    world-building; teeth will come out more in S2)

SUMMARY:
Tyler woke up hungover on Jake Hennessey's couch to a wrong-feeling 
morning — muted news crawl, a town too quiet, a figure moving badly 
down the street. A call from Dave Cormier at the fire hall ("come now, 
come quiet, don't stop for anyone") kicked off the session in earnest. 
At the hall, Dave, Gail, and an unidentified civilian delivered the 
short version: something is spreading through bites, the noise draws 
them, twelve people reached by phone out of two thousand. Tyler 
connected the dots on his own — then immediately panicked about his 
mom. A tense three-block round trip to Harbour Road retrieved Barb 
MacAulay from her basement, where she'd been hiding since 1AM after 
Tyler's father turned overnight. Father's status: infected, still 
in the house, location within house unknown. The group made it back 
to the hall via back alleys with one close call — a recycling bin, 
a frozen ten-count, footsteps that moved away. Everyone intact.

CHARACTER MOMENTS:
- Tyler's first priority on waking: water, then immediately coffee. 
  Set his character immediately — practical, habitual, not yet afraid.
- Chose to text his mom rather than knock at the basement door. 
  Smartest single tactical decision of the session.
- The frozen stare at the four infected before following Dave — 
  couldn't look away. The car-crash effect. Very human, very Tyler.
- The grin at Dave's "how much did you drink" question — and how fast 
  it died when Dave didn't grin back. The moment the tone shifted.

WORLD STATE:
- Dunmore population ~2,000. Twelve confirmed survivors reached 
  by phone as of ~7AM Day 1.
- Outbreak appears to have escalated rapidly overnight. 
  Majority of town compromised.
- Noise confirmed to attract infected — observed twice.
- Harbour Road: Tyler's family home, front door left open, 
  father infected and loose inside, dad's truck in driveway (potential 
  vehicle, keys unknown).
- Fire hall: secured, supplied for 4–5 days, radio contact with 
  unknown number of other survivors.
- Car alarm running somewhere northeast of the hall — 
  persistent, unresolved, likely drawing infected.
- Jake Hennessey: whereabouts unknown. Wallet and keys left behind. 
  Window in his bedroom was open.

LOOSE THREADS:
- Who is the man in the fleece vest? What does he know and 
  why is he at the fire hall?
- Jake Hennessey — friend, host, missing. Window was open.
- Tyler's father — infected, armed with unknown household items, 
  loose in the family home.
- The twelve survivors — who are they, where are they, 
  are any of them coming to the hall?
- The car alarm — it's been running all morning. 
  What's around it?
- Tyler's phone is nearly dead. No charger found yet.
- The fire truck is an asset and a liability. 
  Will there come a moment it's worth the noise?
===========================
```
